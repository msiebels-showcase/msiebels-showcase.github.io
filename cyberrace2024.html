Cisco European CyberRace 2024

17.12.2024

In a two-hour sprint round, we had to work as a team of four to put our cybersecurity skills to the test against Europe-wide competitors. The competition was held in a “Capture the Flag” format and was based on courses such as Networking Basics , Endpoint Security , Network Defense and more.

Technical content:

  • Wi-Fi 6 / 6A,
  • Cisco Catalyst,
  • General information on the McLaren and Cisco cooperation,
  • F1 and McLaren Team,
  • ThousandEyes and Webex.
